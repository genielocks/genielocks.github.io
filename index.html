✦ Under the Hood ✦

HOW THE GENIE THINKS

01 · Its Own Number

For each fighter the Genie builds a rating from win %, finish rate, age, reach and ranking, then turns the matchup into a win probability — with market odds deliberately left out so it forms an independent opinion.

02 · The Market

Live American odds are converted to implied probability and de-vigged to strip the book's margin.

03 · Conditions

For hot, humid or outdoor cards it docks older, high-mileage fighters and rewards fresher legs — a card-specific tweak.

04 · The Edge

edge = genie − market. Straight bets rank by edge; the parlay stacks the best value for a bigger payout.
