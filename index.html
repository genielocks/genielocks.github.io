🧞 How the Genie thinks

Its own number. For each fighter the Genie builds a rating from win %, finish rate, age (decline after ~34), reach, and ranking — then turns the matchup into a win probability. Market odds are left out so it forms an independent opinion.

The market's number. Live American odds are converted to implied probability and de-vigged.

Conditions. For outdoor / hot-humid cards the Genie also docks older, high-mileage fighters (cardio fades in the heat) and rewards fresher legs — a deliberate, card-specific tweak.

The edge. edge = genie_prob − market_prob. Straight Bets rank by edge (best value). The Parlay stacks the best-value legs for a bigger payout — aggressive, with just a light confidence floor so the legs aren't pure longshots.

Honest limits: the fighter stats here are approximate and hand-entered for this card, so treat the output as a smart conversation-starter, not a validated edge.
